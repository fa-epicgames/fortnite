FORTNITE OG is BACK and here to stay. Drop into Chapter 1 and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG gameplay.

THE OG OG RETURNS
Solos or Squads, drop into battle with Fortnite OG: Chapter 1. Build your way to pure victory in Battle Royale or dominate the Island in a new way with Zero Build. Each Fortnite OG season runs approximately two months, bringing back the legendary loot and features from back in the day.

CLASSIC COOL IN THE OG PASS
The Fortnite OG Pass features retro rewards with a modern twist. A new OG Pass will be available every OG season for V-Bucks or in Fortnite Crew, which also includes the Battle Pass, as well as the Music and LEGO Passes.

Getting this experience is not necessary to access this content. When you launch Fortnite using this through your Library, the experience will be pre-selected for your convenience.
